Preparations Before Vaccine

The success of vaccines is strongly influenced by the
strength of the body's immune system. Therefore, there are
several things that can be tried to make the COVID-19
vaccine work :

Alcohol can lead to dependence and alcoholism, it also increases depression and anxiety.

Stress has a profound effect on immune work. Moreover, prolonged stress can increase cortisol production and oxidative stress in the body

A healthy diet is essential for good health and nutrition. Eating a variety of foods and consuming less salt, sugars and saturated and industrially-produced trans-fats.

Sleep helps your body and brain function properly. A good night's sleep can help improve your learning, memory, decision-making, and even your creativity. What's more, getting insufficient sleep has been linked to a higher risk of conditions such as heart disease, diabetes, and obesity.

Regular physical activity, such as walking, cycling, wheeling, doing sports or active recreation, provides significant benefits for health. Some physical activity is better than doing none. By becoming more active throughout the day in relatively simple ways, people can easily achieve the recommended activity levels.
